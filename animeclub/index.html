ABOUT OUR CLUB

This group is only open to MCTC students or people in the MCTC community that are interested in becoming or are already members. We are trying to open the public to anime and show how beneficial it can be positively. We, as a club, would for the most part view and discuss anime and modern Japanese/Asian visual arts. We also play games every now and then. Any other ideas for the club would be great, but our mission is to get other people interested in anime and asian visual arts. Thanks everyone!

OUR VALUES

Openess

We welcome not only current MCTC students, but alunini and other people in the MCTC community - regardless of gender identity, race, religion or any other background.

Education

In order for our members to positivety appreciate anime, we often view and discuss the various anime titles, as well play games related to anime and animated films.

Fun

We encourage everyone to part take in our discussion and join in on the events related to our club.
