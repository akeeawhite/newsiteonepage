    CLASSES

    Bead Bazaar offers classes for jewelry makers at all level. These jewelry classes range from introduction to Jewelry to Metalsmithing to Wirewrapping. Classes are free, so space is limited.To see a list of avaliable classes by month, click on the following links bellow. For more on our classes, please contact Akeea White at 612-483-1917.

    AUGUST CLASSES

    Metalsmithing

    Date & Time: August 6th, 12:00 P.M - 4:00 P.M
    Seat Status: Open (00/20)
    Description: In this course, you will learn how to make metal using various tools such as ring mandrels, torches, and tube cutters, wrap and tap pliers.

    Introduction to Jewelry Making

    Date & Time: August 13th 4th, 12:00 pm - 3:00 pm
    Seat Status: Open (07/30)
    Description: This course will cover the basic techniques of making jewelry from wire wrapping to attaching a chain, and other decorate components.

    Wirewrapping

    Date & Time: August 21st, 12:00 P.M - 2:00 P.M
    Seat Status: Open (19/24)
    Description: You will learn the process of wire wrapping using gauged wire and tools. In addition, you will learn how to attach new links to a chain and other decorative components.

    Stained Glass

    Date & Time: August 28th, 12:00 P.M - 4:00 P.M
    Seat Status: Open (08/20)
    Description: A popular class for people who want to go behind the traditional bead weaving technique. This course teaches you various design techniques using stained glass beads.
